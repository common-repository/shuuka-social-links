Shuuka social link help you to display all your social link in one single page. Your own bio link for instagram and other social media. You need only a shuuka account.
Shuuka social links plugin, is a service plugin from shuuka.com that use shuuka.com links for images.
== Description ==

We see a problem with the various nicknames we often can not use in the different networks the same. It is sometimes complicated to remember all the names of the networks. Our mission is to simplify this for the user and the follower. With Shuuka.com, you can list all networks, making it easy for you and your followers to list your nicknames whenever and wherever you want. For you as a user it is simple and practical. You list all your social media and have a statistics with the visits, clicks and shuks to your followers. We always work diligently to be up-to-date on networks and insist on a simple use for all.

Smart links. 
With Shuuka smart links, users will be able to remember the link easily and intuitively.

Verify all your profiles.
With shuuka you can certify all your profiles so that all your users only follow your official accounts.

Grow Audience.
We help you to find you easily. With our ranking list, they will find your faster, which will lead you to win more followers.


== Installation ==

= INSTALLATION FROM WITHIN WORDPRESS =
Visit Plugins > Add New. 
Search for Shuuka social link. 
Install and activate the Shuuka social links plugin. 
Put your Secret Key from shuuka.com and write a slug on the shuuka social link setting page.

= MANUAL INSTALLATION =
Upload the entire shuuka-social-links folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory. 
Visit Plugins. 
Activate the Shuuka social links plugin. 
Put your Secret Key from shuuka.com and write a slug on the shuuka social link setting page.
